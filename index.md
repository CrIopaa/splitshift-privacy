# Privacy Policy — SplitShift

*Last updated: March 2026*

## Overview

SplitShift is a household chore tracking app for iPhone, designed for couples. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

SplitShift collects only the minimal data needed for the app to function:

- **Display name** — The name you choose to identify yourself to your partner. This is not your real name unless you choose it to be.
- **Household name** — A label you choose for your shared household.
- **Chore log entries** — Category, effort level, timestamp, and optional notes for tasks you log.
- **Partner color** — A color preference for visual identity in charts.
- **Invite code** — A randomly generated code used to pair with your partner.

## How Data Is Stored

SplitShift uses Apple iCloud (CloudKit) to sync data between you and your partner. Your data is stored in Apple's CloudKit infrastructure and is subject to Apple's privacy practices.

A backup of your data is also stored in your personal iCloud account (CloudKit private database and key-value store) so it can be restored if you reinstall the app.

No data is stored on any server we own or operate.

## Sign in with Apple

SplitShift supports Sign in with Apple for authentication. We only receive the anonymous user identifier provided by Apple. We do not receive or store your email address or Apple ID.

## No Third Parties

SplitShift does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. No data is shared with or sold to any third party.

## What Your Partner Can See

When you pair with a partner, they can see your display name, partner color, and chore log entries within your shared household. They cannot see any other data on your device.

## Data Deletion

You can unpair from your partner in Settings, which removes the shared connection. To fully delete your data, remove the app from your device and your iCloud data will be cleared.

## No Tracking

SplitShift does not track you across apps or websites. We do not collect device identifiers, location data, or usage analytics.
